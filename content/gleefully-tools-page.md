# Joey, Phoebe, and the Chai Chase

This is a game, full stop: a midnight chai quest starring two extremely famous cats and the person whose personality inspired the warmth behind the wider Glee-fully world.

**Glee-fully Chai Chasers** is a free browser game built for one person, then opened up for everyone to enjoy. Real cascading reels. A meter that climbs toward something. A wheel you spin. A legendary symbol so rare that landing it is basically a personality trait once it happens to you. It all runs on fictional Glee-coins, and if your balance ever gets low, a very specific co-pilot finds more under the couch and hands them over with a shrug.

## Meet the cast

You already know **Joey and Phoebe** if you've spent any time in this ecosystem, but here they get to be the main characters. Phoebe shows up for literally any treat, no judgment, she's a queen and she knows it. Joey is pickier, obviously, because Joey has standards, and Joey only shows up for his specific treat. If the jar's empty when he wanders by, he looks at the board, looks at you, and leaves. That's canon. That's just who he is.

There's an iced chai tumbler, jewel-toned and mermaid-patterned, doing top-symbol duty on the reels, because if you know, you know: this house does not do hot chai. Around it are music-night keepsakes, books, butterflies, a cardigan, and PNW aurora. A legendary rainbow butterfly called UniGlee appears rarely enough to feel mythical. You'll know it when you see it. You'll probably yell.

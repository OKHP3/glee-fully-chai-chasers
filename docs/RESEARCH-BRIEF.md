## The copyable skeleton

1. **Cascades:** payline wins removed (UFOs "beam up" winners), gravity refill, re-evaluate, repeat.
2. **Consecutive-cascade meter → free spins.** Canonical ladder (Return/Attack): 4→7, 5→10, 6→15, 7→20, 8→50, 9→75, 10→100, 11+→200. (Original title conflicts at 7 cascades: 25 per PlayUSA vs 20 elsewhere — irrelevant, we define our own.)
3. **Stacked wilds** 6-7 high on multiple reels.
4. **Specialty wilds** awarded on line hits, queued one per dead board: Blast Reels (blast 5-11 symbols to force a cascade; wilds immune; extras queue), Drop-In Wilds (wild stack crowns reel), Double Bonus (doubles spin awards), Moolah Bonus (wilds carry credit prizes in free spins).
5. **Unicow archetype:** ultra-rare symbol awarding the full package (Double + Moolah + Drop-In + 3-4 Blasts) — near-guaranteed monster bonus. Player lore: 300+ spin rounds. This legend structure is the retention centerpiece.
6. **Bonus-entry wheel** (Attack): one of three free-spin modifiers — random wild multipliers to 7x, giant symbols, extra falling wilds — plus a jackpot pick screen.
7. **Social casino loop** (Jackpot Party): daily bonus wheel, level/XP unlocks, escalating celebration animation, visible near-miss tension at meter position 3.
